How to Play?
  • Guess the word in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid 4/5/6 letter word based on difficulty.
  • Word will be automatically submitted after 5 characters.
  • After each guess the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • Green color of the tile means the letter and its position both are correct.
  • Yellow color of the tile means the letter is present in the word but at a different position.
